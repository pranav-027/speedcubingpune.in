Competition Guidelines

Registration

Register on the WCA website. Early bird discounts available for most competitions. Check competition-specific requirements.

Equipment

Bring your own cubes. All puzzles must comply with WCA regulations. Backup cubes recommended for important competitions.

Timing

Arrive at least 30 minutes before your first event. Check-in is mandatory. Competition schedule will be shared via email.

Results

Results are live-streamed and uploaded to WCA database. Certificates and prizes awarded at the end of competition day.

Ready to Compete?

Join our community of speedcubers and participate in official WCA competitions. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you!
