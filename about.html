About Speedcubing Pune

Discover our journey, mission, and the amazing people behind our success

Our Story

Speedcubing Pune was founded in 2018 with a simple vision: to create a thriving community of speedcubers in Pune and Maharashtra. What started as a small group of passionate cubers has now grown into one of India's most active and successful speedcubing organizations.

We are an official World Cube Association (WCA) affiliated club, which means all our competitions are recognized globally and contribute to official world rankings. Our competitions attract participants from across India and have helped many cubers achieve their personal bests and national records.

Our Mission

  • Promote speedcubing culture in Maharashtra
  • Organize high-quality WCA competitions
  • Provide learning opportunities for beginners
  • Support competitive cubers in achieving their goals
  • Build a strong and inclusive cubing community
