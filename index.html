What is Speedcubing?

Speedcubing is the hobby of solving twisty puzzles, most commonly the 3×3×3 Rubik's cube, as quickly as possible. Speedcubers use special techniques and algorithms to solve the cube in the shortest time possible.

The sport has grown tremendously over the years, with competitions held worldwide and official records recognized by the World Cube Association (WCA).

World Cube Association (WCA)

The WCA is the worldwide non-profit organization that regulates competitions for mechanical puzzles. It was founded in 2004 and has grown to over 100 countries with more than 180,000 registered competitors.

All our competitions are WCA official, meaning your results count towards official world rankings and records!
